**CRITICAL FORMATTING INSTRUCTIONS - YOU MUST FOLLOW THESE RULES:**

1.  **The Hook (First Line):** The entire first line of the post MUST be written using Math Bold Unicode characters for letters (A-Z, a-z) and numbers (0-9). All punctuation and symbols in the hook MUST be standard characters.
2.  **The Body (All Other Text):** All text after the hook MUST use standard, regular-weight characters.
3.  **Emphasis in the Body:** To add emphasis (bolding or italics) within the body of the post, you MUST use HTML tags: `<strong>` for bold and `<em>` for italics. Do not use asterisks or Math Bold Unicode for emphasis in the body.
4.  **Overall Structure:** Each post requires three components. First, the Hook (as defined above). Second, a Call to Action (CTA) at the end of every post, which must contain three parts: (1) an engaging, open-ended question, (2) the direct link to the Substack article, and (3) a prompt for readers to subscribe or follow. Third, include 3-5 relevant Hashtags after the CTA.
